About Us

A.N.I. Trading House Pvt. Ltd.

A.N.I. Trading House Pvt. Ltd. is a well-established Hardware materials chain operating in Nepal for over 21 years. We are an authorized distributor and dealer for Leading brands like Asian Paints with types of cement, sand, iron rod (Reinforcement), Bricks, Chips, Fine Dust, and many more. We believe in providing a high-quality/quantity product and service. A.N.I. Trading House Pvt. Ltd. is the name of customers trust.
